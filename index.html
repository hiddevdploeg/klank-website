Let's talk about music. We all love listening to music.

Whether you're commuting to work, having a dinner party, exercising in the gym or want to be focussed, there is music for every feeling and occasion.

The tricky part is to find music that matches your mood as it changes during the day.

You feel so much more comfortable if the music matches with your mood.

Yet the only way Spotify and Apple Music are currently trying to solve this is by providing you Stations and Playlists.

Well, I don't know about you, but we don't want to search for a new playlist or station every time I'm in a different mood, that's too difficult and takes too long.

So good news, it is possible because music theory is still the same.

That's why we have been building Klank.

It provides you a slider to change the mood of your music, no matter what time of day.

It's fun, you don't have to think about it, and you'll even discover some great new tunes from time to time.

Klank is like a modern radio station that fits with you for the whole day.
